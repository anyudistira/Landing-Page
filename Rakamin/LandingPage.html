Bekasi: A Whole Another Planet!

Bekasi is a city in West Java, Indonesia, located on the eastern border of Jakarta. It serves as a commuter city within the Jakarta metropolitan area. According to the 2020 Census by Statistics Indonesia, Bekasi had 2,543,676 inhabitants. The official estimate for mid 2022 was 2,590,257.
